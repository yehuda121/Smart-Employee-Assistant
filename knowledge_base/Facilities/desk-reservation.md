# Desk Reservation (Hot-Desking)

## Purpose

This document describes the desk reservation system for Amdocs offices operating hot-desking and hoteling models. Desk reservation ensures employees have guaranteed workspace when visiting the office while optimizing real estate utilization in flexible work environments.

## Scope

This procedure applies to employees assigned to hot-desking offices: Ra'anana Building C, St. Louis West Building, Dublin Sandyford, and Pune Tower 4. Employees with assigned permanent desks (Ra'anana A/B, St. Louis North/South) do not need to reserve unless visiting a hot-desking floor.

## Procedure

1. **Access the desk reservation system.** Navigate to `https://desks.amdocs.internal` or use the **Amdocs Workplace** mobile app (iOS/Android). Authenticate with Amdocs SSO.

2. **Reserve a desk.** Select your office location, date, and preferred floor/zone. The floor map displays available (green), occupied (red), and partially available (yellow) desks. Click an available desk and confirm the reservation. Default reservation: full business day (08:00–18:00 local).

3. **Select desk attributes (optional).** Filter by: dual monitors, standing desk, window seat, proximity to team zone (team neighborhoods are marked on the map), wheelchair accessible, and quiet zone.

4. **Reserve for your team.** Use **Team Booking** to reserve adjacent desks for up to 10 people. Enter team members' email addresses; they receive confirmation and the reservation appears on their calendar.

5. **Check in at the desk.** Scan your badge at the desk QR code or tap **Check In** in the mobile app within 30 minutes of reservation start. Unchecked reservations are released after 30 minutes (same policy as meeting rooms).

6. **Extend or release.** Extend a reservation via the app if the desk is available for the extended period. Release early if leaving before your reservation end to make the desk available for walk-ins.

7. **Walk-in (unreserved) desks.** Desks marked **Walk-In Only** on the map (typically 15% of floor capacity) are available without reservation on a first-come-first-served basis. Check in via QR code upon sitting.

8. **Reserve lockers and storage.** Daily lockers are available on each hot-desking floor. Reserve via the same portal under **Storage > Locker**. Lockers auto-release at end of business day; personal items left overnight are moved to Lost and Found (KB-FAC-009).

## Important Notes

- Maximum advance booking: 4 weeks. Maximum consecutive days: 5 (Mon–Fri). Weekend desk booking requires manager approval.
- Employees may hold one active desk reservation per day. Duplicate bookings across locations are blocked.
- **Neighborhoods:** Teams are assigned neighborhood zones (e.g., "Billing Platform — Zone C3"). Priority booking opens 48 hours earlier for neighborhood members.
- Permanent desk conversion: employees requiring daily office presence may request a permanent desk assignment via manager approval and Facilities review (subject to availability).
- Cleaning: desks are cleaned nightly. Wipe down shared surfaces with sanitizing wipes available at zone supply stations.
- Monitor connections: hot-desking desks have USB-C docking monitors. Bring your laptop; desktop PCs are not provided at hot desks.
