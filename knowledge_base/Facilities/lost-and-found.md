# Lost and Found

## Purpose

This document describes the lost and found process at Amdocs office locations. Following this procedure increases the likelihood of recovering lost personal and company items and ensures unclaimed items are handled in accordance with Amdocs data security and disposal policies.

## Scope

This procedure applies to all items lost or found on Amdocs premises, including personal belongings, Amdocs corporate property, and visitor items. Digital data loss (lost files, credentials) is handled separately by IT Security (KB-SEC-003).

## Procedure

1. **Report a lost item.** Submit a report via the **Lost and Found Portal** at `https://lostfound.amdocs.internal` or visit the reception desk at your office location. Provide: item description, last known location, date/time lost, and contact information. Reports are cross-matched daily against found item logs.

2. **Turn in a found item.** Deliver found items to the reception desk or Facilities office on the same floor where the item was found. Do not leave found items at your desk or in communal areas. For valuable items (laptops, phones, wallets), contact Corporate Security directly at ext. 4910.

3. **Found Amdocs corporate property.** If you find an Amdocs laptop, badge, access token, or corporate credit card, immediately deliver it to Corporate Security (ext. 4910) — do not hold or attempt to identify the owner yourself. Corporate devices may contain sensitive data and require immediate secured handling.

4. **Item logging and storage.** Reception logs found items with description, date found, location, and finder name. Items are stored in secured Lost and Found cabinets at reception. Valuables (electronics, jewelry, wallets over $50 value) are stored in the Corporate Security safe.

5. **Claim a found item.** To claim an item, visit reception with a valid Amdocs badge or government ID. Describe the item; reception verifies against the log. Sign the claim form. Items containing company data (USB drives, documents) require Security clearance before release.

6. **Notification of matches.** When a lost report matches a found item, both parties are notified via email. Unclaimed lost reports remain active for 90 days.

7. **Unclaimed item disposition.**
   - **0–30 days:** Stored at reception/security
   - **30–90 days:** Transferred to central storage; listed on portal under **Unclaimed Items**
   - **After 90 days:** Personal items donated to local charity or discarded. Corporate items returned to IT Asset Management or destroyed per data security policy.

## Important Notes

- Amdocs is not liable for lost or stolen personal items on company premises. Employees are encouraged to use daily lockers in hot-desking areas (KB-FAC-007).
- Lost badges must be reported to Corporate Security immediately (KB-FAC-004) — do not wait to see if the badge is turned in.
- Lost corporate laptops trigger an automatic security incident report and remote wipe regardless of whether the device is recovered.
- Perishable items (food containers, clothing) are disposed of after 7 days.
- High-value found items (>$500) require a witness during handoff and are photographed for the log.
