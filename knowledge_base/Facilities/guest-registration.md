# Guest Registration for Office Visits

## Purpose

This document defines the procedure for registering external guests, clients, vendors, and candidates visiting Amdocs office locations. Guest registration ensures building security, compliance with visitor management policies, and a professional reception experience.

## Scope

This procedure applies to all non-Amdocs personnel entering Amdocs office buildings globally. It includes clients, vendor representatives, job candidates, auditors, and personal guests. Deliveries and courier personnel follow a separate process (KB-FAC-009).

## Procedure

1. **Pre-register guests (required).** At least 2 hours before the visit (24 hours for international guests or visits to Restricted areas), log in to the **Amdocs Visitor Portal** at `https://visitors.amdocs.internal`. Click **Register Guest** and enter:
   - Guest full name, company, email, phone number
   - Visit date, expected arrival time, and duration
   - Amdocs host name (yourself) and department
   - Office location and specific floor/room
   - Visit purpose (Client Meeting, Vendor, Interview, Audit, Other)

2. **Security screening.** The portal automatically checks guest names against the Amdocs denied-access list. Flagged registrations are routed to Corporate Security for manual review (allow 24 hours for clearance).

3. **Guest receives confirmation.** The guest receives an email with: visit confirmation, office address and parking instructions, WiFi voucher (if requested), and a QR code for check-in. For Ra'anana HQ, guests must present government-issued photo ID matching the registration name.

4. **Host responsibilities on arrival day.** Host must: meet the guest in the lobby (guests cannot proceed unescorted beyond the lobby turnstiles), badge in with the guest at reception or self-service kiosk, and escort the guest throughout the visit.

5. **Guest check-in at kiosk.** Guests scan their QR code at the lobby kiosk, scan their ID, and receive a temporary visitor badge (color-coded by access level: green = general areas, yellow = escorted only, red = Restricted area with pre-approval).

6. **NDA and photography.** Guests accessing development areas or meeting rooms with whiteboards displaying project information must sign an NDA at reception (digital signature on kiosk). Photography and recording are prohibited without Marketing/Legal approval.

7. **Guest checkout.** Host escorts guest to lobby at visit end. Guest returns badge to kiosk (auto check-out) or reception desk. Unreturned badges trigger a security alert and may result in host department review.

## Important Notes

- Walk-in guests without pre-registration may be denied entry during high-security periods (quarterly audit windows, announced on intranet).
- Maximum guest capacity per floor is tracked for fire code compliance. The portal will warn if floor capacity is reached.
- Vendor technicians requiring data center or server room access need additional approval from IT Operations and Security (submit via **Visitor Portal > Restricted Access Request**).
- Candidate interviews: Recruiting pre-registers candidates. Hiring managers do not need to duplicate registration unless adding a team lunch or extended visit.
- Personal guests (family, friends) are permitted in lobby and cafeteria areas only, during non-core hours, with manager awareness.
