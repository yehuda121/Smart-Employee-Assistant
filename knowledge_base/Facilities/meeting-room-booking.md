# Meeting Room Booking

## Purpose

This article explains how Amdocs employees reserve meeting rooms at office locations and virtual meeting resources. Effective room management reduces scheduling conflicts, supports hybrid meetings, and optimizes office space utilization.

## Scope

This procedure applies to all meeting rooms at Amdocs office locations globally, including standard conference rooms, executive boardrooms, training rooms, and phone booths. Virtual-only Teams meetings do not require room booking but may use the **Hybrid Meeting Kit** reservation for rooms equipped with Teams Rooms.

## Procedure

1. **Book via Outlook (recommended).** In Outlook calendar, create a new meeting and click **Room Finder** or **Add a Room**. Select your office location (e.g., **Ra'anana HQ**, **St. Louis Campus**) to filter available rooms. Choose a room based on capacity and equipment needs. Rooms auto-decline if the organizer's calendar conflicts.

2. **Book via the Facilities Portal.** Navigate to `https://rooms.amdocs.internal` for advanced features: recurring bookings (up to 12 weeks), multi-room booking for large events, and equipment add-ons (extra microphones, whiteboards on wheels).

3. **Select room type by need:**
   - **Small (2–4 people):** Phone booths and huddle rooms (e.g., RH-S-HUD-01)
   - **Medium (6–10 people):** Standard conference rooms with display and speakerphone
   - **Large (12–20 people):** Conference rooms with Teams Room system and camera
   - **Executive (8–12 people):** Boardrooms requiring director+ sponsorship (RH-EXEC-01, STL-EXEC-01)
   - **Training (20–50 people):** Require 5 business days advance booking and Facilities approval

4. **Configure hybrid meeting setup.** For rooms with Teams Room, add `TeamsRoom-{RoomName}@amdocs.com` as a room resource. The in-room console auto-joins the Teams meeting. Test AV 5 minutes before the meeting start.

5. **Check in upon arrival.** Scan your badge at the room tablet within 10 minutes of start time. Unchecked bookings are auto-released after 15 minutes (soft booking policy) to prevent no-shows.

6. **Modify or cancel bookings.** Cancel unused bookings at least 1 hour in advance to free the room for others. Recurring series cancellations must be done in Outlook by editing the series.

7. **Report room issues.** Submit ServiceNow tickets under **Facilities > Meeting Room Issue** for AV failures, connectivity problems, or cleanliness concerns. Urgent AV failures during active meetings: call Facilities hotline at internal ext. 4601.

## Important Notes

- Maximum booking duration: 4 hours for standard rooms, 8 hours for training rooms (single day). Multi-day bookings require Facilities approval.
- Personal meetings (non-business) may use rooms outside core hours (before 08:00, after 18:00) with manager approval logged in the portal.
- Catering for meetings >10 people: order through the portal (**Add Services > Catering**) at least 24 hours in advance. Charge to department cost center.
- Room naming convention: `{Site}-{Floor}-{Type}-{Number}` (e.g., RH-3-CONF-07 = Ra'anana, Floor 3, Conference Room 7).
- Do not rearrange permanent AV equipment. Whiteboard contents should be erased after meetings.
