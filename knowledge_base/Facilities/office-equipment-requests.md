# Office Equipment Requests

## Purpose

This document outlines how Amdocs employees request office equipment and supplies beyond standard IT hardware (which follows KB-IT-004 and KB-IT-013). This includes furniture, ergonomic accessories, office supplies, and shared team equipment for Amdocs office and approved home office locations.

## Scope

This procedure applies to all Amdocs employees requesting non-IT office equipment. IT hardware (laptops, monitors, docks) is handled by IT Asset Management. Facilities handles furniture, ergonomic items, whiteboards, team equipment, and general office supplies.

## Procedure

1. **Identify your need.** Determine the equipment category:
   - **Ergonomic:** Monitor arms, footrests, keyboard trays, lumbar supports
   - **Furniture:** Additional chairs, bookshelves, mobile whiteboards, privacy screens
   - **Team equipment:** Team-branded signage, presentation clickers, label makers
   - **Office supplies:** Stationery, toner (for personal desk printers), cleaning supplies
   - **Home office (approved remote):** Desk, chair, filing cabinet (permanent remote only)

2. **Check availability in the Facilities Catalog.** Browse `https://facilities.amdocs.internal/catalog` for pre-approved items available for immediate request. Standard items (monitor arm, footrest, whiteboard markers) ship within 2 business days.

3. **Submit a request.** Create a ServiceNow ticket under **Facilities > Office Equipment Request**. Include: item description, quantity, delivery location (desk number or home address), business justification, and cost center (CC-XXXX-DEPT).

4. **Approval workflow.**
   - Catalog items under $200: auto-approved
   - Non-catalog items or $200–$1,000: manager approval
   - Items over $1,000: manager + department budget owner approval
   - Home office furniture: permanent remote status verified in Workday + manager approval

5. **Delivery and setup.** Facilities delivers and assembles items at your desk or home office within 5–10 business days of approval. Large furniture (bookshelves, filing cabinets) requires 10–15 business days. You will receive a delivery notification with a scheduled window.

6. **Return or transfer equipment.** When changing desks, departments, or leaving the company, do not move Facilities-owned furniture without submitting a **Transfer Request** in ServiceNow. Home office furniture must be returned upon termination (KB-HR-005).

7. **Office supply replenishment.** Each floor has supply closets stocked weekly. For standard supplies (pens, notebooks, sticky notes), visit the supply closet. For bulk or specialty orders, submit via the Facilities Catalog or ServiceNow.

## Important Notes

- Standard desk setup includes: one chair, one monitor (hot-desking: at desk; assigned desk: one per person), one keyboard/mouse set. Additional items require justification.
- Ergonomic assessments: employees experiencing discomfort should request a virtual ergonomic evaluation at `ergonomics@amdocs.com` before ordering equipment. Assessments may recommend specific catalog items at no cost.
- Standing desks are available in hot-desking zones (reserve via KB-FAC-007). Permanent standing desk conversion requires ergonomic assessment recommendation.
- Amdocs-branded items (banners, team plaques) must follow Brand Guidelines (`https://brand.amdocs.internal`). Marketing approval required for external-facing signage.
- Equipment remains Amdocs property. Personal decoration (plants, photos, personal mugs) is permitted within reason but is not reimbursable.
