# Projector and AV Equipment Usage

## Purpose

This document provides guidelines for using projectors, displays, and audiovisual (AV) equipment at Amdocs office locations. Proper usage and care of AV equipment ensures reliable presentations, reduces repair costs, and supports professional client and internal meetings.

## Scope

This procedure covers permanently installed AV equipment in meeting rooms, portable projectors and screens available for checkout, and personal display adapters issued by Facilities. It applies to all Amdocs employees and registered guests using AV equipment on Amdocs premises.

## Procedure

1. **Use built-in room AV (preferred).** Most Amdocs conference rooms (Medium and Large categories, KB-FAC-002) include ceiling-mounted projectors or large displays, ceiling speakers, and wireless presentation systems (Mersive Solstice or Barco ClickShare). Turn on the display using the wall panel or table touch controller.

2. **Connect your device wirelessly.** Select the room's wireless presentation option on the touch panel. On your laptop, connect to the room's Solstice/ClickShare SSID or launch the Solstice app (pre-installed on Amdocs devices). Enter the on-screen PIN to begin presenting. Wired HDMI connections are available as backup in all Medium and Large rooms.

3. **Check out portable equipment.** For rooms without built-in AV or events in training rooms, reserve portable equipment via the Facilities Portal (`https://rooms.amdocs.internal > Equipment Checkout`). Available items:
   - Epson portable projectors (1080p, HDMI)
   - Portable projection screens (60" and 80")
   - Wireless presentation adapters (USB-C and HDMI)
   - Portable speaker systems
   - Video conference cameras (Logitech Rally Bar)

4. **Pick up and return equipment.** Collect checked-out equipment from the Facilities storage room (location listed in reservation confirmation) within 30 minutes of reservation start. Return equipment by the reservation end time. Late returns incur a $25 fee and may affect future checkout privileges.

5. **Operate projectors safely.** Allow ceiling-mounted projectors 30 seconds to warm up before switching inputs. Do not look directly into the projector lens. Power off using the wall panel (not by unplugging). Portable projectors: run cooldown cycle (fan continues for 60 seconds after power off — do not move or unplug during cooldown).

6. **Request AV support for critical meetings.** For client presentations, executive meetings, or town halls, request dedicated AV technician support via ServiceNow (**Facilities > AV Support Request**) at least 2 business days in advance. Include room, date/time, attendee count, and technical requirements.

7. **Report malfunctions.** If equipment fails before or during a meeting, call AV Support at internal ext. 4601 for immediate assistance. Submit a ServiceNow ticket for non-urgent repairs.

## Important Notes

- Do not install personal software on room PC systems (where present). Use your laptop for all presentations.
- Supported presentation formats: HDMI (preferred), USB-C DisplayPort Alt Mode, wireless (Solstice/ClickShare). VGA adapters are no longer provided — request a USB-C hub from IT if needed.
- Volume levels: keep conference room door closed during presentations exceeding 70 dB to avoid disturbing adjacent workspaces.
- Marking pens: only use Amdocs-provided dry-erase markers on whiteboards. Permanent marker damage charges apply to the booking department ($150 cleaning/replacement).
- External events (industry meetups hosted at Amdocs) require Facilities and Corporate Communications approval before AV equipment use.
