# Amdocs Office Locations

## Purpose

This reference document provides Amdocs employees with key information about global office locations, including addresses, primary functions, facility contacts, and site-specific policies. Accurate location information supports travel planning, visitor coordination, and cross-site collaboration.

## Scope

This document covers all Amdocs-owned and major leased office facilities globally. Remote employees without an assigned office should use their employment country location for HR and facilities purposes. Data center locations are not listed (Restricted access — contact IT Operations).

## Procedure

## Important Notes

- **Hot-desking locations:** Ra'anana Building C, St. Louis West Building, and Dublin operate hot-desking models. Reserve desks via KB-FAC-007 before visiting.
- **Inter-office travel:** Employees traveling to another Amdocs office should notify the destination site Facilities team 24 hours in advance for desk and badge access provisioning.
- **Time zones:** Amdocs operates across IST, IST+2.5 (Israel), CET, GMT, CST, EST, and PST. Use the **Amdocs Time Zone Converter** on the intranet for scheduling.
- **Office closures:** Weather-related or emergency closures are announced via the Amdocs Alert System (SMS + email). Register your mobile at `https://emergency.amdocs.internal`.
- New offices and closures are announced quarterly in the **Facilities Newsletter** on the intranet.
