# Building Access Cards and Badge Management

## Purpose

This article describes how Amdocs employees obtain, use, replace, and return building access badges. Badge management is a critical security control that governs physical access to Amdocs offices, labs, and restricted areas.

## Scope

This procedure applies to all Amdocs employees, long-term contractors, and approved vendors requiring regular building access. Temporary visitor badges are covered in KB-FAC-003 Guest Registration. Badge technology varies by location: RFID (Ra'anana, Pune), HID Prox (St. Louis), and mobile badge (Dublin pilot program).

## Procedure

1. **Receive your initial badge.** New employees receive a temporary badge on Day 1 from office reception or the Facilities desk. The permanent badge is ready within 3 business days after your photo is captured in Workday (auto-synced to the badge system).

2. **Activate badge access levels.** Default access includes: main entrance turnstiles, assigned floor, cafeteria, and common areas. Additional access (labs, server rooms, executive floors) requires separate requests via ServiceNow (**Facilities > Badge Access Request**) with business justification and area owner approval.

3. **Use your badge correctly.** Tap or swipe at turnstiles and door readers. Do not tailgate — each person must badge individually. Badge readers log all entries for security audit. Report turnstile malfunctions to reception immediately.

4. **Request access modifications.** When changing departments, floor assignment, or project (requiring lab access), submit a badge access update in ServiceNow. Old access is revoked within 24 hours of the change effective date.

5. **Report lost or stolen badges immediately.** Contact Corporate Security at corp-security@amdocs.com or internal ext. 4910. The badge is deactivated within 15 minutes. Submit a replacement request via ServiceNow (**Facilities > Badge Replacement**). Replacement cost: $25 payroll deduction (waived for theft with police report).

6. **Replace damaged badges.** Bring the damaged badge to the Facilities desk for a free replacement. Damaged badges must be surrendered — do not discard.

7. **Mobile badge (Dublin pilot).** Dublin employees may opt into the Amdocs Mobile Badge program via the **Amdocs Access** app (iOS/Android). Enroll through the Facilities Portal. Mobile badge functions identically to physical badge and is auto-revoked upon device unenrollment from MDM.

8. **Return badge upon departure.** On your last working day, return your badge to HR or Facilities during offboarding (KB-HR-005). Unreturned badges incur a $100 charge and trigger a security incident report.

## Important Notes

- Badges are company property and must not be lent, duplicated, or modified. Badge cloning is a terminable offense.
- After-hours access (before 07:00, after 20:00, weekends) requires manager-approved extended access flag. Default badge hours: 07:00–20:00 local time, Monday–Friday.
- Restricted area access (data centers, security operations center) requires annual recertification by the area owner.
- Visitors must never use employee badges for building entry. Host is accountable for guest escort compliance.
- Photo updates: submit a new photo in Workday **Personal > Photo**; badge reprints within 5 business days.
