# Parking Requests at Amdocs Offices

## Purpose

This document describes how Amdocs employees request and manage parking allocations at company office locations. Parking management ensures fair allocation of limited spaces, compliance with local regulations, and support for employees with accessibility needs.

## Scope

This procedure applies to Amdocs employees who require parking at the following facilities with managed parking: Ra'anana HQ (Building A and B garages), St. Louis campus (Lot C and D), Pune Hinjawadi campus, and Dublin Sandyford office. Street parking and third-party lots are not managed by Amdocs Facilities.

## Procedure

1. **Check parking availability.** View current availability on the **Amdocs Parking Portal** at `https://parking.amdocs.internal`. Each location displays total spaces, waitlist length, and estimated wait time for new assignments.

2. **Submit a parking request.** Log in to the portal with Amdocs SSO. Click **Request Parking**, select your primary office location, vehicle make/model/color, license plate number, and vehicle type (Standard, Electric/Hybrid, Motorcycle, Accessible).

3. **Select parking type.** Options vary by location:
   - **Assigned (monthly):** Dedicated spot, $45–$75/month payroll deduction depending on location
   - **Unassigned (daily):** First-come-first-served, $5/day via mobile app
   - **EV Charging:** Limited spots with Level 2 chargers; $15/month surcharge (Ra'anana and St. Louis only)

4. **Waitlist processing.** If no spaces are available, you are added to the waitlist automatically. Average wait time: Ra'anana 6–8 weeks, St. Louis 2–4 weeks, Pune 1–2 weeks. You receive email notification when a space opens.

5. **Activate parking access.** Upon assignment, your Amdocs badge is activated for garage entry at RFID gates. For locations using LPR (License Plate Recognition), ensure your plate is registered correctly in the portal. Temporary QR codes are issued for the first 48 hours while badge sync completes.

6. **Manage your allocation.** Update vehicle information, switch between assigned and unassigned, or cancel parking via the portal. Cancellation requires 15-day notice; monthly fee applies through the notice period.

7. **Report parking issues.** For gate malfunctions, unauthorized vehicles in your assigned spot, or damage, contact Facilities via ServiceNow (**Facilities > Parking Issue**) or the parking hotline posted at each garage entrance.

## Important Notes

- Accessible parking spaces require upload of a valid disability placard or medical documentation. Accessible spaces are never waitlisted.
- Carpool parking: Ra'anana offers 20 reserved carpool spots (2+ occupants) at 50% reduced rate. Register carpool members in the portal.
- Overnight parking is permitted in assigned spots only. Unassigned daily parking must exit by 22:00 local time.
- Amdocs is not responsible for theft or damage to vehicles. Personal items should not be left in parked cars.
- Electric vehicle charging is limited to 4 hours per session during business hours to ensure turnover.
