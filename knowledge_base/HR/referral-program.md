# Employee Referral Program

## Purpose

This document describes the Amdocs Employee Referral Program (ERP), which incentivizes employees to recommend qualified candidates for open positions. Referrals are a primary hiring channel at Amdocs and help attract talent aligned with company culture and technical requirements.

## Scope

This program is open to all active Amdocs full-time and part-time employees (excluding HR Recruiting team members for positions within their own requisition portfolio). Referral bonuses apply to eligible requisitions posted on the Amdocs Careers portal (`https://careers.amdocs.com`).

## Procedure

1. **Find eligible openings.** Browse open requisitions on the internal referral portal at `https://refer.amdocs.internal` or the external careers site. Requisitions marked with the **Referral Bonus Eligible** tag qualify for monetary rewards. Executive roles (VP+) and intern positions are excluded.

2. **Submit a referral.** Log in to the referral portal with your Amdocs credentials. Click **Refer a Candidate**, enter the candidate's name, email, LinkedIn profile (optional), and the requisition ID. Upload the candidate's resume if available. You may refer a candidate to up to 2 open requisitions simultaneously.

3. **Candidate notification.** The referred candidate receives an email from `talent@amdocs.com` acknowledging the referral and inviting them to apply. The referral is linked to their application when they submit it within 30 days.

4. **Recruiting process.** Referred candidates follow the standard interview process but receive priority review within 5 business days of application. The referring employee is not involved in interview panels or hiring decisions for their referral.

5. **Track referral status.** Monitor your referrals on the portal dashboard. Status updates include: Applied, In Review, Interviewing, Offer Extended, Hired, Not Selected. You receive email notifications at each stage change.

6. **Bonus eligibility and payment.** Referral bonuses are paid after the referred employee completes 90 days of employment:
   - **Standard roles:** $2,000 USD (or local equivalent)
   - **Hard-to-fill technical roles** (marked with ★ on portal): $5,000 USD
   - **Senior/Principal engineer and above:** $3,500 USD
   Bonus is processed through payroll and taxed as income.

7. **Duplicate referral resolution.** If two employees refer the same candidate, the first submitted referral (timestamp in the portal) receives credit. HR Recruiting makes the final determination in case of disputes.

## Important Notes

- Self-referrals are not permitted. Referring a candidate you previously interviewed as an Amdocs hiring manager within 12 months is disallowed.
- Referral bonus is forfeited if the referred employee is terminated for cause within the first 12 months or if the referrer has left Amdocs before the 90-day milestone.
- There is no cap on the number of referrals per employee, but only 4 bonus payments are processed per employee per calendar year.
- Referring a current contractor for conversion to full-time employee qualifies for 50% of the standard bonus.
- Anti-nepotism policy: referrers cannot be the hiring manager or skip-level manager for direct reports or family members.
