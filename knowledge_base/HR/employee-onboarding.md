# Employee Onboarding

## Purpose

This document outlines the end-to-end onboarding process for new Amdocs employees from offer acceptance through the first 90 days. Structured onboarding ensures new hires are productive quickly, integrated into Amdocs culture, and compliant with all IT, HR, and security requirements.

## Scope

This procedure applies to all new full-time and part-time Amdocs hires globally. Contractors follow a shortened onboarding track (KB-HR-009). Rehires within 12 months receive expedited onboarding with some steps waived.

## Procedure

1. **Pre-boarding (before Day 1).** After accepting the offer in Workday, new hires receive a welcome email from `onboarding@amdocs.com` with:
   - Workday credentials and first-login instructions
   - Background check and I-9/documentation upload links (region-specific)
   - Day 1 agenda and office location or remote setup guide
   - Amdocs Code of Conduct acknowledgment (must sign before Day 1)

2. **Day 1 — Account and access setup.** IT provisions the corporate laptop (shipped to home for remote hires or waiting at the office). New hires complete: Okta MFA enrollment (KB-IT-006), VPN setup (KB-IT-001), email configuration (KB-IT-008), and Teams/Zoom installation. IT Service Desk holds virtual onboarding sessions at 09:00 and 14:00 in each region.

3. **Day 1 — HR orientation.** HR Operations conducts a 2-hour virtual orientation covering: benefits enrollment (must complete within 30 days), payroll setup (direct deposit, tax forms), vacation and sick leave policies, and the Amdocs organizational structure. Calendar invite sent from `hr-ops@amdocs.com`.

4. **Week 1 — Manager onboarding.** The hiring manager conducts a 1:1 to review role expectations, 30-60-90 day plan, team introductions, and project assignments. Manager assigns an onboarding buddy (tenured peer) for informal guidance.

5. **Week 1 — Mandatory training.** Complete the following in the Amdocs Learning Portal within 5 business days:
   - **ONB-100:** Welcome to Amdocs
   - **SEC-100:** Information Security Awareness
   - **COMP-100:** Code of Conduct and Anti-Harassment
   - Role-specific courses as assigned

6. **Week 2–4 — Department integration.** Attend department all-hands, complete role-specific system access requests (GitLab, SharePoint, production — as applicable), and schedule 1:1s with cross-functional stakeholders identified in the 90-day plan.

7. **Day 30, 60, 90 check-ins.** Manager conducts structured check-in meetings at each milestone using the **Onboarding Checklist** template (Workday > Onboarding Dashboard). HR sends automated surveys at Day 30 and Day 90.

8. **Benefits enrollment deadline.** Must complete benefits selection in Workday within 30 days of start date. Late enrollment requires qualifying life event or wait until next open enrollment (November).

## Important Notes

- New hires do not receive production access until completing PROD-101 and SEC-205 (typically Week 2–3 for engineering roles).
- Badge and building access: office-based hires receive a temporary badge on Day 1; permanent badge within 3 business days (KB-FAC-004).
- Payroll first cycle: new hires starting before the 15th of the month receive their first paycheck on the last business day of the same month.
- Relocation assistance (if applicable) is coordinated by `relocation@amdocs.com` separately from standard onboarding.
- Feedback: new hires can report onboarding issues anonymously via the Day 30 survey or directly to `onboarding@amdocs.com`.
