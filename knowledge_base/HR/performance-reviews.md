# Performance Review Process

## Purpose

This document describes the Amdocs annual performance review cycle, including goal setting, mid-year check-ins, year-end evaluations, and calibration. The process ensures consistent, fair assessment of employee contributions and alignment with Amdocs business objectives.

## Scope

This procedure applies to all Amdocs full-time and part-time employees who have completed at least 90 days of employment before the review cycle start date. Contractors and interns are excluded. Employees on extended leave (>90 days during the cycle) receive prorated reviews.

## Procedure

1. **Annual cycle timeline.** The Amdocs performance year runs January 1 – December 31. Key milestones:
   - **January–February:** Goal setting for the new cycle
   - **July:** Mid-year check-in
   - **October–November:** Self-assessment and manager evaluation
   - **December:** Calibration sessions and final ratings
   - **January (next year):** Compensation decisions communicated

2. **Set goals (Q1).** In Workday, navigate to **Performance > Goals**. Create 3–5 SMART goals aligned with team OKRs and Amdocs corporate priorities. Categories: **Business Impact**, **Innovation**, **Collaboration**, **People Development**. Manager must approve goals by February 28.

3. **Mid-year check-in (July).** Manager and employee hold a 45-minute discussion reviewing goal progress. Update goal status in Workday (On Track, At Risk, Completed, Deferred). No formal rating at mid-year; focus on feedback and course correction.

4. **Self-assessment (October).** Employee completes the self-assessment form in Workday by October 31. Include accomplishments, challenges, development areas, and goal achievement evidence with specific metrics where possible.

5. **Manager evaluation (November).** Manager writes the performance evaluation in Workday, assigning a rating on the 5-point Amdocs scale: **Exceptional (5)**, **Exceeds Expectations (4)**, **Meets Expectations (3)**, **Partially Meets (2)**, **Does Not Meet (1)**. Ratings require supporting examples and must be submitted by November 30.

6. **Calibration.** Directors and VPs conduct calibration sessions in early December to ensure rating consistency across teams. Managers may be asked to adjust ratings based on cross-team comparison.

7. **Review delivery (December).** Manager schedules a 1:1 to deliver the final rating, share written feedback, and discuss development plans for the next cycle. Employee acknowledges the review in Workday.

8. **Development planning.** Based on the review, create or update the **Individual Development Plan (IDP)** in Workday. Link development activities to Amdocs Learning Portal courses, mentoring, or stretch assignments.

## Important Notes

- **Rating distribution guidance:** Target distribution is approximately 10% Exceptional, 25% Exceeds, 55% Meets, 7% Partially Meets, 3% Does Not Meet. Individual teams may vary with VP approval.
- Performance ratings directly influence annual merit increases and bonus eligibility. Bonus pool allocation occurs in January.
- Employees who disagree with their rating may submit a formal appeal to their HR Business Partner within 10 business days of acknowledgment. Appeals are reviewed by skip-level management.
- New hires (<90 days) receive a "Not Rated" designation and are eligible for prorated bonus at manager discretion.
- 360-degree feedback is optional and initiated at manager discretion via Workday **Feedback > Request Feedback**.
