# Internal Training Requests

## Purpose

This article explains how Amdocs employees request, enroll in, and track completion of internal and external training programs. Continuous learning is a core Amdocs value, and this procedure ensures training investments align with business needs and budget controls.

## Scope

This procedure covers all training delivered through the Amdocs Learning Portal (ALP), department-led workshops, external conferences, certifications, and tuition reimbursement programs. Mandatory compliance training (security, code of conduct) is auto-assigned and not covered here.

## Procedure

1. **Browse available training.** Access the Amdocs Learning Portal at `https://learning.amdocs.com` via Okta SSO. Browse catalogs: **Technical Skills**, **Leadership Development**, **Product Training (CES, Revenue Management)**, **Compliance**, and **Soft Skills**.

2. **Enroll in internal courses.** For ALP courses, click **Enroll** on the course page. Manager approval is required for courses exceeding 8 hours or with a listed cost. Self-paced courses under 8 hours are auto-approved.

3. **Request external training.** For conferences, vendor certifications (AWS, Azure, PMP), or university courses, submit a **Training Request Form** in Workday under **Career > Learning > Request External Training**. Include: event/course name, dates, cost breakdown (registration, travel, accommodation), and business justification linked to IDP or team goals.

4. **Approval workflow.** External training approval chain:
   - Manager approval (all requests)
   - Budget owner approval (cost > $2,000)
   - HR Talent Management review (cost > $5,000 or exceeding 5 days away from work)

5. **Complete pre-work and attend.** Block your calendar for the training duration. For live virtual sessions, ensure VPN and Teams/Zoom are functional. In-person external training requires travel approval (KB-FIN-002).

6. **Complete post-training requirements.** Within 5 business days of completion:
   - Mark the course complete in ALP (auto-tracked for internal courses)
   - Submit a **Knowledge Share Summary** (1-page template) to your team wiki
   - For certifications, upload the certificate to Workday **Career > Credentials**

7. **Training reimbursement.** External training costs approved in advance are reimbursed via Concur (KB-FIN-008) or paid directly by Amdocs if using the corporate card (KB-FIN-003). Pre-approved tuition reimbursement is processed through Workday **Pay > Tuition Reimbursement**.

## Important Notes

- Annual training budget per employee: $3,500 (technical roles) / $2,000 (non-technical). Budget tracked in Workday and resets January 1.
- Certification exam attempts: one retake covered per certification per year. Third attempts require VP approval.
- Amdocs-sponsored certifications (AWS Solutions Architect, CKA, PMP) have dedicated cohort programs announced quarterly — check ALP before individual registration.
- Training during business hours is expected and should not require compensatory overtime.
- Employees who leave Amdocs within 12 months of company-funded certification may be required to reimburse pro-rated certification costs.
