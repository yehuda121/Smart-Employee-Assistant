# Vacation Request Procedure

## Purpose

This document describes how Amdocs employees request, track, and manage paid vacation (annual leave) through Workday. Proper vacation planning ensures adequate team coverage, compliance with local labor regulations, and accurate payroll processing across Amdocs global locations.

## Scope

This procedure applies to all full-time and part-time Amdocs employees eligible for paid time off (PTO). Vacation accrual rates and carryover limits vary by country of employment. This article describes the global process; country-specific rules are listed in the Workday **Leave Policies** library.

## Procedure

1. **Check your vacation balance.** Log in to Workday at `https://wd5.myworkday.com/amdocs` and navigate to **Time Off > Balance**. View available days, pending requests, and accrual rate. Full-time employees accrue 22–25 days annually depending on tenure and location.

2. **Plan with your team.** Discuss planned absence dates with your manager and team at least 2 weeks in advance (4 weeks for absences exceeding 5 consecutive business days). Update the team shared calendar in Outlook or Teams.

3. **Submit a vacation request.** In Workday, go to **Time Off > Request Absence**. Select absence type **Vacation (Paid)**. Enter start and end dates, total hours/days, and optional comment. Workday calculates business days automatically based on your work schedule and local holidays.

4. **Manager approval workflow.** Your direct manager receives the request in their Workday inbox. Managers must approve or deny within 3 business days. Requests exceeding 10 consecutive days require skip-level manager (director) approval.

5. **Receive confirmation.** Upon approval, you receive a Workday notification and calendar invite block is suggested (add manually to Outlook). Your remaining balance updates immediately.

6. **Modify or cancel requests.** To change dates, withdraw the existing request in Workday (if not yet started) and submit a new one. For cancellations after leave has begun, contact your manager and HR Operations.

7. **Handover responsibilities.** Before departure, complete a handover document (template on the Amdocs Wiki: **Templates > Leave Handover**) and assign coverage for critical tasks in Jira/ServiceNow.

## Important Notes

- **Blackout periods:** Product release windows (published quarterly on the DevOps calendar) may restrict vacation for release-critical roles. Check with your manager.
- **Carryover limits:** Unused vacation exceeding the carryover cap (typically 5 days, varies by country) is forfeited on March 31 each year. Israel-based employees: see local policy for 90-day carryover.
- **Vacation buyout:** Employees in the US and Canada may sell up to 5 unused vacation days during the November open enrollment window.
- Vacation cannot be taken during the first 90 days of employment except with HR Business Partner approval.
- Public holidays do not count against vacation balance. Refer to the **Amdocs Holiday Calendar** on the intranet for your location.
