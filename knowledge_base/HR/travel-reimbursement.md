# Travel Reimbursement

## Purpose

This document outlines the procedure for submitting travel expense reimbursements at Amdocs after completing approved business travel. Proper expense reporting ensures timely reimbursement, audit compliance, and accurate cost allocation to projects and cost centers.

## Scope

This procedure applies to all Amdocs employees who incur out-of-pocket business travel expenses. It covers airfare, lodging, meals, ground transportation, and incidental travel costs. Expenses charged to the Amdocs corporate credit card follow KB-FIN-003 and do not require separate reimbursement unless personal card was used by exception.

## Procedure

1. **Ensure travel was pre-approved.** All business travel must have a pre-approved travel request in Concur before departure (KB-FIN-002). Reimbursements without a linked travel authorization may be denied or delayed.

2. **Retain receipts.** Collect itemized receipts for all expenses exceeding $25 USD (or local equivalent). Credit card statements alone are not acceptable. Required receipt details: vendor name, date, amount, and items purchased.

3. **Log in to Concur.** Access SAP Concur at `https://concur.amdocs.com` via Okta SSO. Navigate to **Expense > Create New Report**. Name the report using convention: `{LastName}_{Destination}_{MonthYear}` (e.g., `Cohen_London_May2026`).

4. **Add expense line items.** For each expense, enter: date, category (Airfare, Hotel, Meals, Ground Transport, Parking, Other), amount in original currency (Concur auto-converts), vendor, and business purpose. Attach receipt images (photo or PDF).

5. **Apply per diem where applicable.** Meals during travel may use Amdocs per diem rates instead of individual receipts. Select **Per Diem** in Concur; rates are auto-populated by city (e.g., London: $75/day, Tel Aviv: $65/day, St. Louis: $55/day). Per diem and itemized meal receipts cannot be mixed on the same day.

6. **Allocate to cost center/project.** Assign each expense to your department cost center (CC-XXXX-DEPT) and, if applicable, a project code (PRJ-XXXX). Split allocations are supported for multi-project trips.

7. **Submit for approval.** Submit the expense report within 30 days of return. Approval chain: manager → finance reviewer (for reports > $2,000). Processing time: 5–7 business days after final approval.

8. **Receive reimbursement.** Reimbursement is deposited to the bank account on file in Workday. Track payment status in Concur under **Expense > Process Reports**.

## Important Notes

- **Non-reimbursable expenses:** personal entertainment, airline upgrades (unless pre-approved for flights >6 hours), minibar charges, traffic violations, pet care, and laundry (except trips >7 days).
- **Airfare class:** Economy for flights under 6 hours; Premium Economy approved for flights 6–10 hours; Business Class requires VP approval (flights >10 hours).
- **Hotel cap:** Maximum $250/night (US/EU), $180/night (APAC/LATAM) unless city rate in Concur exceeds cap. Pre-approved conference hotel blocks are exempt.
- Currency: submit in original currency; Concur converts at OANDA daily rate on expense date.
- Late submissions (>30 days): require director approval. Submissions >90 days are auto-rejected.
