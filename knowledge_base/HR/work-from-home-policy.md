# Work From Home Policy

## Purpose

This policy establishes the guidelines, eligibility criteria, and approval process for Amdocs employees working from home or other non-office locations. It balances employee flexibility with operational requirements, collaboration needs, and information security obligations.

## Scope

This policy applies to all Amdocs full-time and part-time employees globally. Contractors and interns are not eligible unless explicitly stated in their engagement contract. Country-specific legal requirements supersede this policy where they provide greater employee protection.

## Procedure

1. **Understand work arrangement types.** Amdocs recognizes three remote work categories:
   - **Hybrid (Standard):** Up to 3 days per week remote, minimum 2 days in office. Default for most roles post-2024.
   - **Permanent Remote:** Full-time remote with no assigned desk. Requires VP approval and role suitability assessment.
   - **Occasional Remote:** Ad-hoc remote days (e.g., waiting for delivery, minor illness). Manager notification only; no formal approval needed (max 12 days/year).

2. **Submit a hybrid or permanent remote request.** In Workday, navigate to **Career > Request Work Arrangement Change**. Select the desired arrangement type, proposed schedule (days in office vs. remote), and business justification. Attach manager endorsement if submitting proactively.

3. **Complete the approval workflow.** Hybrid requests: manager approval only. Permanent remote: manager → director → VP → HR Business Partner → Facilities (desk release). Approval timeline: 10 business days.

4. **Set up your remote workspace.** Upon approval, follow KB-IT-014 Remote Work Setup for IT configuration and home equipment requests. Permanent remote employees receive the monthly stipend ($75/month, auto-processed).

5. **Adhere to remote work expectations.** Remote employees must: maintain regular working hours per local timezone, be reachable on Teams during core hours (10:00–16:00 local), attend in-person meetings when required, and comply with all information security policies (VPN, MFA, clean desk).

6. **Change or revoke arrangement.** To return to full in-office work or change hybrid days, submit an updated request in Workday. Desk assignment is handled by Facilities (KB-FAC-007). Permanent-to-hybrid transitions require 30-day notice.

7. **International remote work.** Working from a country other than your employment country is not permitted without prior written approval from HR, Legal, and Tax teams. Submit an **International Remote Work Request** via `international-hr@amdocs.com` at least 30 days before travel.

## Important Notes

- Roles requiring on-site infrastructure access (data center ops, lab engineers, facilities staff) are ineligible for permanent remote.
- Managers may mandate office attendance for team events, client meetings, or onboarding weeks with 5 business days notice.
- Remote work is not a substitute for sick leave or vacation. Employees on PTO must not be expected to respond to work communications.
- Performance evaluation criteria are identical for remote and in-office employees.
- Amdocs reserves the right to revoke remote work arrangements due to performance issues, policy violations, or business needs with 30-day notice (60-day for permanent remote).
