# Employee Benefits Overview

## Purpose

This knowledge base article provides Amdocs employees with an overview of available employee benefits, enrollment procedures, and key contacts. Benefits packages are designed to support employee health, financial security, and work-life balance across Amdocs global locations.

## Scope

This overview applies to all full-time Amdocs employees. Part-time employees (20+ hours/week) receive prorated benefits in eligible regions. Contractors and interns are not eligible unless mandated by local law. Specific plan details vary by country; US employees refer to the **US Benefits Guide**, Israel employees to the **IL Benefits Guide** (available in Workday > Benefits > Plan Documents).

## Procedure

1. **Review available benefits.** Log in to Workday and navigate to **Benefits > View All Plans**. Available categories include:
   - **Health Insurance:** Medical, dental, vision (plan options vary by region)
   - **Retirement:** 401(k) with company match (US: 100% match up to 6%), Keren Hishtalmut (Israel), Provident Fund (India)
   - **Life & Disability:** Basic life insurance (2x annual salary), short-term and long-term disability
   - **Wellness:** Gym reimbursement ($50/month), EAP (Employee Assistance Program), annual health screening
   - **Financial:** Employee Stock Purchase Plan (ESPP, 15% discount), Health Savings Account / Flexible Spending Account (US)
   - **Time Off:** Vacation, sick leave, parental leave, bereavement, jury duty (see respective KB articles)

2. **Enroll during onboarding.** New hires must complete benefits enrollment in Workday within 30 days of start date. Default coverage applies if no election is made (medical: basic plan, life: standard coverage).

3. **Make changes during open enrollment.** Annual open enrollment occurs every November 1–15 for coverage effective January 1. All employees may add, change, or drop plans during this window without qualifying life events.

4. **Report qualifying life events.** Outside open enrollment, changes are permitted within 30 days of: marriage, divorce, birth/adoption, death of dependent, or spouse employment change. Submit changes in Workday under **Benefits > Report Life Event** with supporting documentation.

5. **Access plan details and ID cards.** Digital insurance cards are available in Workday **Benefits > Insurance Cards** and through carrier mobile apps (US: Anthem, Delta Dental; Israel: Clalit/Meuhedet options). Physical cards are mailed within 10 business days of enrollment.

6. **Use wellness benefits.** Gym reimbursement: submit monthly receipts via Concur under category **Wellness > Gym/Fitness**. EAP: call 1-800-ADM-HELP (24/7, confidential counseling). Annual health screening: schedule via `https://wellness.amdocs.com`.

7. **Manage retirement contributions.** Update 401(k)/retirement contribution percentages in Workday **Pay > Retirement Savings** or through the plan provider portal (US: Fidelity at `netbenefits.fidelity.com`).

## Important Notes

- **Parental leave:** 16 weeks paid primary caregiver, 8 weeks secondary caregiver (US). Israel: per Sick Funds statute. India: 26 weeks maternity per Maternity Benefit Act.
- ESPP enrollment windows: June and December. Minimum contribution: 1% of base salary; maximum: 15%.
- Domestic partner coverage is available in US, Canada, UK, and Israel with affidavit of domestic partnership.
- Benefits terminate on the last day of the month following separation date. COBRA continuation available (US) — details in offboarding packet (KB-HR-005).
- Benefits questions containing personal health information should be directed to carriers or EAP, not shared via email with HR general inbox.
