# Sick Leave Policy and Reporting

## Purpose

This article defines the procedure for reporting sick leave at Amdocs and outlines employee entitlements, documentation requirements, and manager responsibilities. Accurate sick leave reporting ensures employee wellbeing, team coverage, and compliance with regional labor laws.

## Scope

This procedure applies to all Amdocs employees across all regions. Sick leave entitlements, pay rates, and medical certificate requirements vary by country. Employees must refer to their country-specific addendum in Workday **Leave Policies** in addition to following this global procedure.

## Procedure

1. **Notify your manager promptly.** Contact your direct manager by phone, Teams, or email as early as possible on the first day of illness — ideally before your scheduled start time. If your manager is unavailable, notify their delegate or your HR Business Partner.

2. **Update your status.** Set your Microsoft Teams status to **Out Sick** and configure an out-of-office auto-reply in Outlook if the absence may exceed one day. Include your expected return date if known and a backup contact.

3. **Report sick leave in Workday.** Log in to Workday and navigate to **Time Off > Report Absence**. Select **Sick Leave (Paid)** or **Sick Leave (Unpaid)** as applicable. Enter the date(s) of absence. For same-day reporting, submit by end of business day.

4. **Provide medical documentation when required.** A medical certificate (doctor's note) is required for:
   - Absences exceeding 3 consecutive business days (all regions)
   - Absences exceeding 1 day (Germany, France, Israel)
   - Any absence where workers' compensation may apply
   Upload documentation to Workday under **Personal > Documents > Medical Certificates**.

5. **Extended sick leave.** For absences expected to exceed 10 business days, contact HR Operations to discuss short-term disability benefits, return-to-work plans, and FMLA/ local equivalent eligibility. HR will assign a Leave Coordinator.

6. **Return to work.** On your first day back, notify your manager and close the sick leave entry in Workday if the return date differs from the originally reported date. For absences exceeding 5 days, a fitness-for-duty clearance may be required.

7. **Manager responsibilities.** Managers must acknowledge sick leave in Workday within 24 hours, ensure team coverage, and never request details of the employee's medical condition (only expected duration and work impact).

## Important Notes

- Amdocs sick leave balances: typically 10 days/year (US), unlimited paid sick days first 10 days (Israel), per statutory minimums (EU). Check Workday for your exact entitlement.
- Do not use vacation days to cover illness. Misclassification affects insurance and disability claims.
- Contagious illness: follow local health authority guidance. Amdocs encourages staying home when symptomatic even if remote work is possible.
- Mental health days are covered under sick leave in all Amdocs regions. No separate category is required.
- Patterns of Monday/Friday absences or frequent short absences may trigger a supportive check-in from HR (not disciplinary by default).
