# Remote Work Setup

## Purpose

This guide provides Amdocs employees with the complete setup procedure for establishing a secure and productive remote work environment. It consolidates IT, security, and facilities requirements for employees working from home or non-Amdocs locations on a regular or occasional basis.

## Scope

This procedure applies to all Amdocs employees approved for remote work under the Work From Home Policy (KB-HR-003). It covers initial setup for new remote workers and reconfiguration for employees transitioning from office-based to remote work. Contractors follow a limited subset (VPN and MFA only).

## Procedure

1. **Confirm remote work approval.** Verify your remote work arrangement is documented in Workday under **My Profile > Work Arrangements**. Permanent remote status requires VP approval; occasional remote (up to 3 days/week) requires manager approval only.

2. **Verify hardware readiness.** Ensure you have an Amdocs-managed laptop (KB-IT-004). Request a home office equipment bundle via ServiceNow (**Hardware > Remote Work Kit**) including: external monitor (27" Dell), USB-C dock, wireless keyboard/mouse, and ergonomic chair (optional, manager approval required).

3. **Establish secure connectivity.** Install and configure GlobalProtect VPN (KB-IT-001). Verify MFA enrollment (KB-IT-006). Test connectivity to internal resources: email, Teams, GitLab, and SharePoint.

4. **Configure collaboration tools.** Install and sign in to Microsoft Teams, Zoom (Amdocs licensed), and the Amdocs Wiki. Set your Teams status to reflect remote work location. Enable Teams notifications for chat and meeting reminders.

5. **Set up home network security.** Connect via a password-protected WPA3 or WPA2 router. Do not use public WiFi for Amdocs work without VPN. IT Security recommends a dedicated work VLAN if your router supports it. Document your home network setup in the optional **Remote Work Security Checklist** (`https://security.amdocs.internal/remote-checklist`).

6. **Configure physical workspace.** Ensure adequate lighting for video calls, a door or divider for privacy during confidential calls, and a stable desk surface. Amdocs ergonomics guidelines are available on the HR portal. Request a virtual ergonomic assessment via `ergonomics@amdocs.com`.

7. **Register your remote location.** Update your work location in Workday and the Emergency Contact system (`https://emergency.amdocs.internal`) so Amdocs can reach you during business continuity events or regional emergencies.

8. **Complete remote work orientation.** New remote employees must complete **RW-101: Remote Work Security and Productivity** in the Amdocs Learning Portal within the first week.

## Important Notes

- Remote work stipend: employees in permanent remote arrangements receive $75/month (processed via payroll) for internet and utilities. No receipt submission required.
- Home office equipment remains Amdocs property and must be returned upon termination or return-to-office.
- Printing Restricted documents at home is prohibited unless a approved home printer is deployed (KB-IT-009).
- Working from international locations outside your employment country requires prior HR and Legal approval (tax and labor law implications).
- IT Service Desk walk-up support is unavailable remotely; use ServiceNow, phone, or chat for all IT issues.
