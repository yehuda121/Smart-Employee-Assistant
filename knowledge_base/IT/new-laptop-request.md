# New Laptop Request

## Purpose

This procedure guides Amdocs employees through requesting a new laptop or replacing an existing corporate device. It ensures hardware is standardized, properly configured, and delivered with required security controls before the employee begins using it for Amdocs work.

## Scope

This process applies to all Amdocs employees eligible for a corporate laptop under the IT Equipment Policy. Standard eligibility includes full-time employees, part-time employees working 20+ hours per week, and contractors on assignments exceeding six months. Interns receive devices through a separate streamlined process (KB-IT-0067).

## Procedure

1. **Determine your hardware tier.** Amdocs assigns laptops based on role classification in Workday:
   - **Tier 1 (Standard):** Dell Latitude 5540 — business analysts, HR, finance, admin
   - **Tier 2 (Developer):** Dell Precision 5570 or MacBook Pro 14" (M3) — engineers, architects, QA
   - **Tier 3 (Power User):** Dell Precision 7670 or MacBook Pro 16" (M3 Max) — data scientists, video production, executive staff

2. **Submit a hardware request.** Log in to ServiceNow and select **IT Services > Hardware > New Laptop Request**. Enter your Employee ID, preferred platform (Windows or macOS, if Tier 2/3), delivery location (office or home address), and business justification if requesting a non-standard configuration.

3. **Obtain manager approval.** Your manager receives an approval notification. Non-standard configurations (additional RAM, dual monitors bundled, etc.) require additional approval from IT Asset Management.

4. **Await provisioning.** The IT Endpoint team configures your device with the Amdocs standard image, including GlobalProtect VPN, Okta Verify, Microsoft 365, CrowdStrike Falcon, and BitLocker/FileVault encryption. Standard delivery time is 5–7 business days for office pickup and 7–10 business days for home shipment.

5. **Schedule onboarding session.** You will receive a calendar invite for a 30-minute **Device Handoff Session** with an IT technician (virtual or in-person). During this session, you will sign the Asset Assignment Form, complete initial login, and register your device in the Amdocs MDM (Jamf for Mac, Intune for Windows).

6. **Return existing device.** If replacing a laptop, return the old device within 10 business days of receiving the new one. Drop off at your local IT service window or use the prepaid return label included with home shipments. Wipe confirmation is handled by IT; do not attempt to wipe the device yourself.

7. **Verify application access.** After handoff, confirm access to email, VPN, Teams, and role-specific tools. Submit software installation requests (KB-IT-005) for any additional applications not included in the standard image.

## Important Notes

- Amdocs laptops remain company property. Personal use is permitted within Acceptable Use Policy limits (KB-SEC-007).
- Devices are refreshed on a 3-year cycle. Early replacement requires justification (damage, performance issues, role change) and manager approval.
- Lost or stolen devices must be reported immediately to security@amdocs.com and the Service Desk. Remote wipe will be initiated within 30 minutes.
- International shipments may require 3–5 additional business days and are subject to local import regulations.
