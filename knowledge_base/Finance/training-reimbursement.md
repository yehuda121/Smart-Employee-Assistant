# Training Reimbursement

## Purpose

This document describes the procedure for requesting reimbursement of approved training and professional development expenses at Amdocs. Training reimbursement supports employee growth while ensuring costs align with business needs and budget availability.

## Scope

This procedure applies to external training expenses including conference registration, certification exam fees, tuition for degree programs, and professional membership dues. Internal training through the Amdocs Learning Portal is free and does not require reimbursement. Pre-approved training follows KB-HR-007.

## Procedure

1. **Obtain pre-approval before incurring costs.** All external training expenses require advance approval via Workday **Career > Learning > Request External Training** (KB-HR-007). Do not register or pay for training before approval is confirmed. Retroactive approval is granted only for emergencies with VP authorization.

2. **Eligible expenses for reimbursement:**
   - Conference and workshop registration fees
   - Certification exam fees (first attempt and one retake per year)
   - Required course materials (books, lab fees)
   - Tuition for approved degree programs (see tuition reimbursement below)
   - Professional association membership dues (job-related)
   - Travel expenses for approved training (follows KB-FIN-002 travel approval)

3. **Submit reimbursement in Concur.** After completing the training:
   - Create an expense report within 30 days (KB-FIN-001)
   - Expense type: **Training & Development**
   - Attach: registration confirmation, receipt/payment proof, certificate of completion, and the Workday training approval reference number
   - Assign to the cost center specified in the training approval

4. **Tuition reimbursement program.** Amdocs offers tuition reimbursement for job-related degree programs:
   - **Eligible programs:** Bachelor's, Master's, PhD, and accredited professional certificates
   - **Annual cap:** $10,000 USD (or local equivalent)
   - **Requirements:** minimum grade B or Pass; program must be pre-approved in Workday; employee must remain at Amdocs for 12 months after reimbursement or repay pro-rated amount
   - **Submission:** Workday **Pay > Tuition Reimbursement** with transcript and receipt (within 60 days of course completion)

5. **Certification reimbursement.** Approved certifications (AWS, Azure, PMP, CKA, etc.) are reimbursed upon passing:
   - Exam fee reimbursed within 30 days of passing
   - One retake covered per certification per year
   - Study materials up to $200 per certification
   - Upload certificate to Workday **Career > Credentials**

6. **Approval and payment.** Training reimbursements follow standard Concur approval routing (KB-FIN-001). Tuition reimbursements are reviewed by Talent Management and processed in the next payroll cycle (bi-weekly).

## Important Notes

- **Non-reimbursable:** Training unrelated to current or planned role, personal interest courses, meals during local training, parking at local events, and training already covered by the department training budget via direct payment.
- **Tax implications:** Tuition reimbursement over $5,250/year (US) may be taxable. Finance provides tax guidance annually.
- **Conference attendance:** Registration fee is reimbursable; travel requires separate travel approval (KB-FIN-002). Presenting at a conference (speaker) has expanded reimbursement — contact Talent Management.
- **Bonding agreement:** Tuition reimbursement over $5,000/year requires a 12-month retention agreement. Departing employees repay on a pro-rated basis.
- **Department direct pay:** For conferences over $2,000, departments may pay the vendor directly via PO (KB-FIN-009) instead of employee reimbursement.
