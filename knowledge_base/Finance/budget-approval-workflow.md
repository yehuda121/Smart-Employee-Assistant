# Budget Approval Workflow

## Purpose

This document describes the Amdocs budget planning, submission, and approval workflow. The budget process ensures financial resources are allocated strategically across departments and projects, aligned with Amdocs corporate objectives and revenue forecasts.

## Scope

This procedure applies to all Amdocs departments, cost centers, and project budgets. It covers annual budget planning, mid-year revisions, and ad-hoc budget transfers. Individual expense approvals within an approved budget follow KB-FIN-001 and KB-FIN-004.

## Procedure

1. **Annual budget cycle timeline.**
   - **August:** Finance publishes budget guidelines, templates, and revenue assumptions
   - **September:** Departments draft budgets in the FinOps Planning module
   - **October:** Department reviews with Finance Business Partners
   - **November:** Executive review and consolidation
   - **December:** Board approval and budget finalization
   - **January 1:** New budget year effective

2. **Prepare department budget.** Department heads and finance liaisons access the FinOps Planning module at `https://finops.amdocs.internal/planning`. Budget categories include:
   - **Personnel:** Headcount, salaries, benefits, contractors
   - **Operations:** Travel, training, office expenses, subscriptions
   - **Capital:** Equipment, infrastructure, software licenses (capitalized)
   - **Project:** Initiative-specific costs linked to PRJ codes

3. **Submit for review.** Complete all budget line items with monthly phasing (not just annual totals). Submit through FinOps by the September 30 deadline. Late submissions are included in the next review cycle only if capacity permits.

4. **Finance Business Partner review.** Each department has an assigned Finance BP who reviews submissions for: alignment with corporate targets, reasonableness of assumptions, comparison to prior year actuals (+/- 15% variance requires explanation), and headcount reconciliation with HR workforce plan.

5. **Executive approval cascade.**
   - Department head → VP → CFO (for departments)
   - Project budgets → Project sponsor → PMO → VP → CFO
   - Capital expenditures over $500K → CFO → CEO → Board (if over $2M)

6. **Mid-year budget revisions.** Submit revision requests in FinOps under **Budget > Revision Request** with justification. Revisions are reviewed monthly:
   - Reallocation within department (same VP): department head approval
   - Cross-department transfer: both department heads + Finance BP
   - New unbudgeted spending: VP + CFO approval

7. **Monitor budget utilization.** Track actual vs. budget monthly in FinOps dashboards. Managers receive automated alerts at 75%, 90%, and 100% utilization for each budget line. Overspend without approved revision blocks new purchase requisitions in Ariba.

## Important Notes

- Budget granularity: minimum line item level is cost center + GL account + month. Project budgets require PRJ code linkage.
- Contingency: departments may hold up to 5% of total budget as unallocated contingency with VP approval.
- Headcount budget is managed jointly with HR. Unfilled positions accrue savings tracked in FinOps but do not auto-transfer to operational budget.
- Multi-currency: budgets are set in USD. Local currency departments see converted amounts at the Finance-published annual exchange rate.
- Zero-based budgeting is applied to discretionary categories (travel, training, events) every 3 years (next cycle: 2027).
