# Vendor Request Process

## Purpose

This document outlines the procedure for requesting new vendor onboarding at Amdocs. Vendor management ensures that third parties meeting Amdocs business needs also meet security, legal, financial, and compliance requirements before engagement.

## Scope

This procedure applies to all new vendor relationships where Amdocs will exchange money, data, or services. It covers software vendors, professional services, hardware suppliers, consultants, and cloud service providers. Existing vendor additions (new products from approved vendors) follow an abbreviated process.

## Procedure

1. **Determine if a new vendor is needed.** Before initiating vendor onboarding, verify:
   - The product/service is not available from an existing Amdocs vendor (check Approved Vendor List)
   - An internal Amdocs solution cannot fulfill the need
   - The spend justifies the vendor onboarding effort (minimum expected annual spend: $5,000)

2. **Submit a Vendor Request Form.** In SAP Ariba, navigate to **Sourcing > Vendor Request**. Provide:
   - Vendor company name, website, and primary contact
   - Product/service description and business justification
   - Estimated annual spend and contract duration
   - Data classification of information to be shared with vendor (KB-SEC-002)
   - Cost center and project code

3. **Complete parallel review processes.** Vendor onboarding triggers simultaneous reviews:

   | Review | Team | Timeline | Focus |
   |---|---|---|---|
   | Financial | Finance/Procurement | 5 days | Credit check, financial stability, tax compliance |
   | Legal | Legal Department | 10 days | Contract terms, liability, IP, regulatory compliance |
   | Security | Information Security | 10 days | Data handling, SOC 2/ISO 27001, penetration test results |
   | Privacy | Privacy Office | 5 days | GDPR/CCPA compliance, DPA requirements |

4. **Provide vendor documentation.** The requesting employee must facilitate collection of:
   - W-9 (US) or equivalent tax form
   - Certificate of Insurance
   - SOC 2 Type II report (for vendors handling Confidential+ data)
   - Company registration documents
   - References (for services >$50,000/year)

5. **Negotiate contract terms.** Legal drafts or reviews the contract using Amdocs standard templates. Key terms: data processing agreement (if PII involved), SLA, liability caps, termination clauses, and auto-renewal opt-out.

6. **Vendor approval and setup.** Upon all reviews passing, Procurement creates the vendor record in SAP and Ariba. The vendor receives onboarding instructions for the Amdocs Supplier Portal (`https://suppliers.amdocs.com`).

7. **Ongoing vendor management.** Vendor performance is reviewed annually. Vendors handling Restricted data undergo annual security reassessment. Requesting employee is the **Vendor Owner** responsible for the relationship.

## Important Notes

- Vendor onboarding average timeline: 3–6 weeks (simple) to 3– months (complex, international, Restricted data).
- **Prohibited vendors:** Vendors in countries under comprehensive sanctions (OFAC list) are blocked. Legal must approve vendors in partially sanctioned regions.
- Shadow vendors (engaging vendors without onboarding) is a compliance violation. Expenses from non-approved vendors will be rejected.
- Vendor consolidation: Procurement may redirect requests to existing preferred vendors offering equivalent services at better terms.
- Vendor offboarding: when ending a vendor relationship, submit a Vendor Termination Request to ensure data return/destruction and access revocation.
