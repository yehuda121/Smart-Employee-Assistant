# Corporate Credit Card Usage

## Purpose

This document outlines the policies and procedures governing the use of Amdocs-issued corporate credit cards (Visa Commercial cards issued through JP Morgan). Corporate cards streamline business purchasing while maintaining financial controls, audit trails, and compliance with Amdocs spending policies.

## Scope

This policy applies to all Amdocs employees issued a corporate credit card and their managers. Cards are issued based on role requirements: standard cards for managers and above, project cards for project leads, and executive cards for VP+ with higher limits. Not all employees are eligible.

## Procedure

1. **Request a corporate card.** Submit a ServiceNow request under **Finance > Corporate Card Request**. Provide business justification, estimated monthly spend, and cost center. Approval: manager → Finance (for limits over $5,000/month).

2. **Receive and activate the card.** Cards are mailed to your home address within 7–10 business days. Activate by calling the number on the card sticker or via the JP Morgan portal at `https://commercial.jpmorgan.com`. Set up online account access linked to your `@amdocs.com` email.

3. **Use the card for approved business expenses only.** Permitted categories:
   - Business travel (airfare, hotel, ground transport, meals)
   - Client entertainment
   - Office supplies and equipment under $500
   - Software subscriptions (with SAM approval, KB-IT-015)
   - Conference registration fees
   - Approved vendor invoices under $2,500

4. **Prohibited charges.** Do not use the corporate card for:
   - Personal purchases of any kind
   - Cash advances
   - Gift cards or prepaid cards
   - Alcohol (except client entertainment with pre-approval)
   - Employee gifts or rewards (use HR programs)
   - Political contributions or charitable donations (use Corporate Social Responsibility program)

5. **Reconcile monthly.** Corporate card transactions auto-import into Concur within 3 business days. By the 10th of each month, complete your Concur expense report for all prior month charges:
   - Attach receipts for every transaction
   - Assign cost center and project code
   - Add business purpose
   - Submit for manager approval

6. **Handle disputes.** For incorrect charges, contact the merchant first. If unresolved, file a dispute through JP Morgan within 60 days. Notify `card-services@amdocs.com` of all disputes.

7. **Report lost or stolen cards.** Call JP Morgan immediately (number on back of card) and notify `card-services@amdocs.com`. A replacement card is issued within 3 business days.

8. **Return upon departure.** Surrender the corporate card to Finance during offboarding (KB-HR-005). Outstanding balances must be reconciled before your last day.

## Important Notes

- **Credit limits:** Standard: $5,000/month. Project: $15,000/month. Executive: $50,000/month. Temporary limit increases require Finance approval.
- **Personal use, even with intent to reimburse, is strictly prohibited.** Accidental personal charges must be repaid to Amdocs within 5 business days (payroll deduction if not reimbursed).
- **Late reconciliation:** Failure to submit Concur reports by the 10th triggers a reminder. By the 20th, the card is suspended. By the 30th, the card is cancelled and escalated to HR.
- **Audit:** Finance audits 100% of executive card transactions and 10% random sample of all other cards quarterly.
- **Tax implications:** Corporate card benefits are not taxable if used exclusively for business. Personal use creates taxable income reporting obligations.
