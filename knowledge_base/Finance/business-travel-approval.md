# Business Travel Approval

## Purpose

This document defines the pre-approval process for all Amdocs business travel. Advance authorization ensures travel aligns with business objectives, budget availability, and duty-of-care obligations before employees incur non-refundable expenses.

## Scope

This procedure applies to all Amdocs employees traveling for business purposes, including client visits, conferences, internal meetings at other Amdocs sites, training events, and vendor meetings. Local commuting to your assigned office is not business travel. Remote employees traveling to an Amdocs office follow this procedure.

## Procedure

1. **Determine travel necessity.** Before booking, confirm the travel objective cannot be achieved via Teams/Zoom meeting. Internal meetings at other Amdocs sites may require in-person attendance if specified by the meeting organizer.

2. **Create a travel request in Concur.** Log in to `https://concur.amdocs.com` and navigate to **Travel > Create Travel Request**. Enter:
   - Trip name: `{Purpose}_{Destination}_{Dates}`
   - Destination city and country
   - Travel dates (departure and return)
   - Estimated costs: airfare, hotel, meals, ground transport, registration fees
   - Business justification (linked to project code or OKR)
   - Traveler(s) — add additional travelers if booking for a group

3. **Book through Concur Travel (preferred).** After approval, book flights and hotels through Concur's integrated booking tool (Amdocs corporate rates with American Airlines, Delta, United, Marriott, Hilton). Concur Travel bookings auto-populate the expense report.

4. **Approval workflow.** Travel requests route based on total estimated cost:
   - Under $2,000: manager approval
   - $2,000–$10,000: manager → director
   - Over $10,000: manager → director → VP
   - International travel to high-risk countries: additional Security and Legal review

5. **Receive approval before booking.** Do not book non-refundable flights or hotels before approval is confirmed in Concur. Emergency travel (family bereavement, critical client escalation) may use retroactive approval with VP authorization.

6. **Travel registration.** Upon approval, your trip is registered in the Amdocs Travel Tracker (`https://travel.amdocs.internal`). The tracker enables duty-of-care notifications (natural disasters, civil unrest, health advisories) to reach you during travel.

7. **Modify or cancel travel.** Update the travel request in Concur before changes. Cancellations should follow airline/hotel cancellation policies. Travel insurance through Amdocs corporate policy (Chubb) covers cancellations for business reasons — contact `travel@amdocs.com` for claims.

8. **Post-travel.** Submit expense report within 30 days of return (KB-FIN-001). Unused per diem or prepaid items must be accounted for.

## Important Notes

- **Advance booking:** Book airfare at least 14 days in advance for economy rates. Last-minute bookings require director approval regardless of cost.
- **Class of service:** Economy (under 6 hours), Premium Economy (6–10 hours, auto-approved), Business Class (over 10 hours or VP approval).
- **Hotel caps:** $250/night (US/EU major cities), $180/night (APAC/other). Concur displays pre-negotiated rates — booking outside negotiated rates requires justification.
- **Companion travel:** Personal companions may accompany business travelers at personal expense. Companion costs must not appear on Amdocs expense reports.
- **Visa and passport:** Employee responsibility for valid travel documents. Amdocs Legal provides invitation letters for business visa applications — request via `travel@amdocs.com` at least 30 days before travel.
