# Invoice Submission for Vendors and Contractors

## Purpose

This document describes how external vendors and Amdocs employees (on behalf of vendors) submit invoices for payment. Standardized invoice processing ensures timely payment, accurate cost allocation, and compliance with Amdocs accounts payable policies.

## Scope

This procedure applies to all vendor invoices for goods and services provided to Amdocs, contractor invoices processed through Amdocs AP, and employee-submitted invoices when acting as a vendor liaison. Employee expense reimbursements follow KB-FIN-001 (Concur), not this procedure.

## Procedure

1. **Vendor invoice submission (preferred method).** Approved vendors submit invoices through the Amdocs Supplier Portal at `https://suppliers.amdocs.com`:
   - Log in with vendor credentials (provisioned during onboarding, KB-FIN-005)
   - Navigate to **Invoices > Submit New Invoice**
   - Enter PO number (mandatory for invoices over $500), invoice date, amount, and line items
   - Upload invoice PDF and supporting documentation

2. **Employee-submitted invoices (on behalf of vendors).** If a vendor cannot use the portal, Amdocs employees may submit via email to `ap-invoices@amdocs.com` with:
   - Vendor invoice (PDF)
   - Amdocs PO number
   - Cost center and project code
   - Manager approval (for non-PO invoices)

3. **Invoice requirements.** All invoices must include:
   - Amdocs legal entity name and address (listed on PO)
   - Vendor name, address, and tax ID
   - Unique invoice number (no duplicates)
   - Invoice date and payment terms
   - PO number (for PO-based purchases)
   - Itemized description, quantity, unit price, and total
   - Applicable taxes (VAT/GST) with tax registration number

4. **Three-way match validation.** Accounts Payable performs automated matching:
   - **PO** (approved purchase requisition)
   - **Receipt** (confirmed delivery in Ariba)
   - **Invoice** (vendor submission)
   All three must align within 5% tolerance. Mismatches are routed to the PO requester for resolution.

5. **Approval routing.** Matched invoices route for approval:
   - Under $5,000: auto-approved if three-way match passes
   - $5,000–$50,000: PO requester (manager) approval
   - Over $50,000: PO requester → director → Finance

6. **Payment processing.** Approved invoices are paid per vendor payment terms (Net 30 default, Net 45 for international). Payment methods: ACH (domestic US), wire transfer (international), check (exception only). Payment date is visible in the Supplier Portal.

7. **Track invoice status.** Vendors track status in the Supplier Portal: Received, In Review, Approved, Scheduled for Payment, Paid. Employees can query status by emailing `ap-invoices@amdocs.com` with the invoice number.

## Important Notes

- **Payment terms:** Amdocs standard is Net 30 from invoice receipt date (not invoice date). Early payment discounts (2/10 Net 30) are accepted when economically beneficial.
- **Duplicate invoices:** Duplicate submissions are auto-rejected. Vendors must reference the original submission number for resubmissions.
- **Non-PO invoices:** Invoices without a PO are accepted only under $500 and require manager + Finance approval. Over $500 without a PO will be rejected.
- **Disputed invoices:** If Amdocs disputes an invoice, AP notifies the vendor within 10 business days with the reason. Disputed amounts are held until resolution.
- **1099 reporting (US):** Vendors receiving $600+ annually receive a 1099 form. Ensure W-9 is on file (collected during vendor onboarding).
