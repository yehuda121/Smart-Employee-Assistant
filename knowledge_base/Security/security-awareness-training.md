# Security Awareness Training

## Purpose

This document describes the Amdocs Security Awareness Training program, including mandatory courses, completion requirements, and supplemental resources. Security awareness is a critical defense layer that complements technical controls by equipping employees to recognize and respond to threats.

## Scope

This program applies to all Amdocs employees and contractors with Active Directory accounts. Training requirements vary by role: all employees complete baseline training; employees with elevated access (production, admin, ISL) complete additional role-based modules.

## Procedure

1. **Complete mandatory baseline training.** All employees must complete the following courses in the Amdocs Learning Portal (`https://learning.amdocs.com`):

   | Course Code | Title | Frequency | Deadline |
   |-------------|-------|-----------|----------|
   | SEC-100 | Information Security Awareness | Annual | Within 30 days of hire, then every January |
   | SEC-110 | Phishing and Social Engineering | Annual | Same as SEC-100 |
   | SEC-120 | Physical Security and Clean Desk | Annual | Same as SEC-100 |
   | COMP-100 | Code of Conduct (includes security module) | Annual | Same as SEC-100 |

2. **Complete role-based training (if applicable).**

   | Course Code | Title | Required For | Frequency |
   |-------------|-------|-------------|-----------|
   | SEC-205 | Handling Customer Data | Production access, customer-facing roles | Annual |
   | SEC-210 | Advanced Phishing Defense | Employees who fail 3+ phishing simulations | One-time |
   | SEC-300 | Secure Development Lifecycle | Software engineers, QA, DevOps | Annual |
   | SEC-400 | Information Security Liaison Training | Designated ISLs | Annual |
   | PROD-101 | Production Access Fundamentals | Production system access | Annual |

3. **Access assigned training.** Log in to ALP via Okta SSO. Assigned courses appear on your **My Learning** dashboard with due dates. Email reminders are sent at 14, 7, and 1 day(s) before deadline from `learning@amdocs.com`.

4. **Complete courses.** Each course includes video modules, interactive scenarios, and a quiz (minimum passing score: 80%). You may retake quizzes unlimited times. Completion is tracked automatically in Workday and ALP.

5. **Phishing simulation program.** Quarterly simulated phishing emails are sent to all employees. Results are tracked by department (not individually published). Departments with click rates exceeding 15% receive additional targeted training.

6. **Non-compliance consequences.** Failure to complete mandatory training by the deadline results in:
   - Day 1 overdue: reminder email to employee and manager
   - Day 7 overdue: VPN access restricted to training portal only
   - Day 14 overdue: all system access suspended until training is completed
   - Day 30 overdue: escalated to HR for disciplinary review

## Important Notes

- New hires must complete SEC-100, SEC-110, SEC-120, and COMP-100 within 30 days of start date (tracked in onboarding dashboard, KB-HR-004).
- Training content is updated annually to reflect current threat landscape. 2026 updates include: AI-generated phishing, deepfake social engineering, and cloud misconfiguration awareness.
- Training completion records are retained for 7 years for audit and compliance purposes.
- Contractors follow the same training requirements; vendor managers ensure compliance.
- Questions about training content: contact security-awareness@amdocs.com. Technical ALP issues: contact learning@amdocs.com.
